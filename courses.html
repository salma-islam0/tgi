Welcome to TGI Program

A bridge cutting-edge research with real-world applications. Whether you are beginning your academic journey or advancing your expertise, our courses combine rigorous content with hands-on learning led by experienced faculty and researchers.

    Program Timeline

  • Pre-Program Before program starts, juniors in each field are devided into groups and meet their mentors to start their research journey.
  • Week 1-4 Participants receive focused coursework in their chosen field, followed by a practical exam to ensure readiness for research.
  • Week 4-6 Participants are introduced to the basics of scientific research, citation styles and how to choose a research topic. Finally, students learn how to structure a research proposal and are guided through submitting their own.
  • Week 7-10 Students progressively build their research papers through structured guidance and weekly tasks. They participate in mentor-led sessions, submit key drafts preparing for final sumbission.
  • Week 11-12 Juniors submit the final version of their research papers. After peer review and modifications, high-quality papers are given the opportunity of publication in our journal
